Prague is the capital and largest city in the Czech Republic, the 14th largest city in the European Union and also the
 historical capital of Bohemia. Situated in the north-west of the country on the Vltava river, the city is home to about
 1.3 million people, while its larger urban zone is estimated to have a population of 2.6 million.[8] The city has a 
 temperate climate, with warm summers and chilly winters.
